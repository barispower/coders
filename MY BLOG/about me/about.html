Polis Academy

Between 1995-1999 I studied in Polis Academy. This is a kind of university that trains managers for the Turkish National Polis Force during 4 years.

Students were used to be accepted after completing Police Colleges or completing high schools when they were 18 years old.

Being stayed in the dormitories, studied on laws, trained to have the perfect skills for managing different kinds of departments in Turkish National Police Force and learnt to use standard kinds of pistols and had a strong physical training in self defence. It's new name is Institue of Security Sciences.
